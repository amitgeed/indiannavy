THE MILITARY ROLE

The essence of all navies is their military character. In fact, the Raison dâ€™etre of navies is to ensure that no hostile maritime power can degrade own national security and interests. The navy's military role is characterized by the threat or use of force at and from the sea. This includes application of maritime power in both offensive operations against enemy forces territory and trade, and defensive operations to protect own forces, territory and trade. The military role is performed through the accomplishment of specific military objectives, missions and tasks.

OBJECTIVES

> Deterrence against war or intervention
> Decisive military victory in case of war
> Security of India's territorial integrity, citizens and off-shore assets from sea-borne threat
> Influence affairs on land
> Safeguard India's mercantile marine and marine and maritime trade
> Safeguard India's national interests and maritime security
